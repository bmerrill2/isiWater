Training

ISI Water chemistries is committed to the safety and education of its employees and customers. We pride ourselves on the background of our representatives and the knowledge that they are able to share with our customers. We will provide as much training at a facility as our customers would like. At a minimum, we provide training on the following:

Cooling Tower Theory and Operation

Cooling Tower Chemical

Boiler Theory and Operation

Boiler Chemicals

Chemical Safety

Testing Procedures

Out of Spec Action

Additionally, our custom reporting software allows for our customers to log testing results online and get notified via email when a test result is out of spec.
