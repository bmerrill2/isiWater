About Us

Everything you need to know about our ISI Water Chemistries Inc.

Who We Are.

ISI Water Chemistries Inc. is a full service water treatment company specializing in cooling tower and boiler water treatment. We service the Utah, Colorado, Wyoming, Idaho, Nevada and Arizona Markets.

What We Do.

We approach your water systems with a service first mentality. We still maintain that the best results come from on-site professional visits and exceptional training. See what our customers are saying about us here

Why choose Us.

Simply put, we get the best results. While other companies gravitate toward less educated tech reps and unreliable automation, we take a technical and educational approach to water treatment, providing you with top notch service at a competitive rate.
