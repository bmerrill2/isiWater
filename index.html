Mission Statement

We are a technical service and sales company, in that order. Our vision is to combine our technical expertise and formulated products with the customer understanding of their facility to accomplish:

  • Maximize longevity of facility water treated equipment
  • Minimize corrective maintenance to water treated equipment
  • Minimize fuel/electricity/water costs for water treated systems
  • Maintain safety for equipment and personnel
  • Minimize environmental impact of water treatment services.

Our water treatment representatives are here to help! Representatives available to cover the following territories: Utah, Nevada, Idaho, Wyoming, Arizona and Colorado.
